Our belief

Built for people
the mainstream
overlooks

Most apps are built for the largest possible audience. We go the other way — finding communities that are underserved, misunderstood, or invisible to mainstream tech, and building tools that actually fit their lives.

We're a solo founder studio. Every product decision, every line of the AI system prompt, every safety rule stays in-house. That's not a constraint — it's what keeps the mission intact.

Good design and genuine empathy aren't luxuries. They're what makes a tool people trust.
