Privacy Policy

Livanelli LLC  ·  Effective March 2026  ·  Philadelphia, PA

We built these apps for people in difficult, sensitive situations. We take that responsibility seriously. This policy explains clearly and plainly what data each app collects, what stays on your device, and what leaves it — with no ambiguity.

We do not sell data. We do not run ads. We do not share your personal information with third parties except as required to deliver the core features described below.

01

Who we are

Livanelli LLC is an independent mobile app studio based in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. We build AI-powered apps for underserved communities. Our contact email is gabrielfethi@livanelli.com.

This policy covers the livanelli.com website and all apps published by Livanelli LLC, currently CoParent Shield and DiaWise.

02

This website

livanelli.com is a static informational site. It does not use cookies, analytics trackers, advertising pixels, or any third-party tracking scripts. No personal data is collected simply by visiting this site.

If you contact us by email, your message and email address are used only to respond to you. We do not store them in any database or CRM.

03

Our apps

CoParent Shield
Launching 2026

What stays on your device

  • Journal entries, incident notes, and image attachments are stored in an encrypted local SQLite database on your device. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to our servers or any third party.
  • Your onboarding preferences and app settings are stored locally in your device's secure storage.

What leaves your device

  • Messages you submit for analysis are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response. Anthropic's data handling practices are governed by their privacy policy at anthropic.com. We do not store the content of your messages on our servers.
  • Basic, anonymous usage signals (e.g. app crashes) may be collected by Apple or Google through standard platform crash reporting. This is controlled by your device's privacy settings.

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any account information in v1.0.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or data brokers — ever.
DiaWise
Coming 2026

DiaWise is currently in development. A detailed privacy policy for DiaWise will be published here before the app becomes available. It will follow the same principles as CoParent Shield: local-first data storage, no data sales, and transparent disclosure of any third-party services used.

04

Third-party services

CoParent Shield uses the following third-party service to deliver its core AI features:

  • Anthropic Claude API — Messages you submit for analysis are processed by Anthropic. Their privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.

We do not use advertising networks, social media trackers, marketing automation platforms, or data brokers in any of our apps.

05

Your rights

Because most data in our apps is stored locally on your device, you are already in control of it. You can delete the app at any time to remove all locally stored data.

If you have questions about data that may have been transmitted (e.g. messages sent to the Anthropic API), or if you would like to make a data request, please contact us at gabrielfethi@livanelli.com and we will respond within 30 days.

06

Children's privacy

Our apps are intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has submitted personal information through one of our apps, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

07

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. For significant changes affecting how we handle sensitive data, we will notify users within the app.
